Recommendations

Through Seed

  • 25 ml Kiecite – G in 10 lit water and soak seeds in it for 25-30 minutes.
  • Remove empty, light weighted, pest infected, malformed and defective seeds.
  • spread the treated seeds for shade drying.
  • Note : Kiecite – G seed treatment improves germination, tends to profuse rooting and healthier crop stand in initial vegetative growth stage of a crop.

Through Soil

  • "NPK dose + Boracol Permite (10 kg) per acre"

Through Spray

  • Spray the crop with 0.5% Kiecite – G / Miczink / Kainase by dissolving 500 ml of product in 100 lit water. First spray should be given 12-15 days after germination.
  • A fine mist spray in early morning hours or late evening is recommended.
  • Take 2-3 subsequent sprays of Kiecite-G with same concentration each at 15 days interval.
  • The last and fourth spray should be given in grain milking stage or pre-maturity stage of a crop.
  • Note : Do not mix insecticide, pesticide or any plant protection chemical with Kiecite-G.
